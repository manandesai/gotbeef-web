Got Beef

We all got beef with someone. Settle yours here.

Got Beef is a social entertainment application where you can settle your personal beefs and help settle the world's beefs through the power of democracy.

Settle all your arguments with your friends ... and even yourself.



Got beef with a friend? Beefs can be private so only your friends and their friends will be able to vote. If you're feeling brave, make your beef public and let the world decide the champion.

Once you start a beef, both sides will be able to make their case and explain why they're right. The more beefs you win, the higher your Beef Score. Climb the ranks and prove to your friends that you're the one who's always right.

Can't make a decision? Got Beef can help with that, too. Start a Self Beef and explain the two sides you're torn between. Then, sit back and let the people decide for you.

Vote on beefs to make your voice heard.



Vote on your friends' beefs and all public beefs to help solve everyone's squabbles. Read both sides of the argument and decide which side you're on.

Can't decide what vote to cast or have more to add? Hop in the comment section and join the conversation. Maybe someone will convince you or make you change your mind.

Start beefing today.
